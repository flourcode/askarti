Trust & Safety

Your Data is Private

Your prompts are sent directly to the AI model for processing and are not stored on our servers. We do not keep a history of your requests or use them for future training.

AI Model Information

AskArti uses Google's Gemini 1.5 AI Model. By default, Google may use API inputs to improve its models. For enterprise customers, data protection policies are available.

How Recommendations Work

Arti is trained on sales methodologies from top-performing sales professionals. It analyzes your situation and provides strategic advice based on proven sales frameworks and tactics that have worked in similar scenarios.

Limitations

While Arti provides valuable guidance, it doesn't know your specific company policies or have access to your internal data. Always use your judgment and align recommendations with your organization's practices and values.

About AskArti

What is AskArti?

AskArti is an AI-powered sales coach designed to help sales professionals strategize and overcome obstacles in their deals. Whether you're dealing with tough objections, complex buying committees, or competitive situations, Arti provides actionable, personalized advice.

Our Methodology

Arti combines multiple proven sales frameworks including MEDDIC, Challenger Sale, SPIN Selling, and Value Selling to provide a comprehensive approach to your unique situation. Our recommendations are based on strategies that have worked for top performers across industries.

Who Should Use AskArti?

AskArti is perfect for:

  • Sales reps stuck on challenging deals
  • Account executives preparing for critical meetings
  • Sales managers coaching their teams
  • SDRs crafting outreach strategies
  • Customer success teams working on expansions

Our Team

AskArti was created by sales professionals for sales professionals. Our team has collective experience across SaaS, enterprise tech, financial services, and healthcare sales, bringing real-world expertise to every recommendation.
